As the founder and President of Divyang First Foundation, I want to share with you the story behind why our organization exists. I am a mother of two wonderful children, one of whom is hearing impaired and the other who is living with cerebral palsy.I know firsthand how difficult it can be to navigate a world that is not always accommodating to those with different abilities. But I also know that with the right support, anything is possible.
To provide comprehensive, holistic support to individuals with disabilities, women, brand senior citizens. Our mission is to promote inclusive education, offer quality medical care, and deliver rehabilitation services. Through the creation of our "Ashram," we aim to offer a safe, nurturing environment where all services—including medical treatment, education, therapy, and vocational training—are available under one roof. Support Us
To create an inclusive society where individuals with disabilities, women, and senior citizens are respected, valued, and provided with equal opportunities to lead fulfilling lives. We aspire to be a leading force in disability and senior care, fostering a world where everyone receives the dignity and support they deserve.
